Scope and limitations

mcPHASES is small and has unequal modality coverage. Utah is larger but is history-only and selected participants with regular bleeding. The cohorts are evaluated independently and their absolute errors are not directly comparable.

This is an independent within-cohort replication, not prospective validation or a test of model transfer between cohorts.

This is an exploratory research benchmark. It is not intended for diagnosis, fertility planning, treatment, individual guidance, or perimenopause prediction.
